About Us

Having a clean car is important. A recent study published that the average American citizen spends close to 2 hours in their car every day. At Chestermere Detailers we offer a comprehensive suite of interior and exterior services to make your car look and feel like it just rolled off the lot! Whether it's pet hair, stains and messes from children, scratched or sun beaten paint, we have the expert staff and professional grade equipment to make your car turn heads wherever you go.

Interior services

Vacuuming - Removes loose dust and debris from all over the interior.

Shampooing - Technicians use a specialized tool to spray then extract a heated cleaning solution into carpets and seats to remove stains, dirt, and odors.

Steam cleaning - High pressure steam is used to remove grime from the inside of buttons and switches making sure they all feel like new, no more sticking.

Leather restoration and conditioning - Our upholstery specialists will fix discoloration, cracks, and tears in your leather seats and trim.

Odor removal - Powerful O-Zone emitters among other special equipment is used to remove odors from headliners, seats, carpets, and Hvac lines.

Seat belt cleaning - Restore the spring in your old dirty seatbelt through a full extraction and sanitization process.

Headliner cleaning - Special compounds and tools are used to gently remove stains and odors from your car's headliner.

UV protection - A UV resistant coating is applied to the dashboard and all interior trim pieces to restore and protect them from the sun.

Exterior services

Foam cannon and hand wash - a pre soak, followed by a foam cannon, then a two bucked hand wash leads to a perfect clean with zero swirl marks.

Waxing - Full application of specialized wax to protect paint and enhance shine.

Wheel and tire restoration - Full removal of brake dust, road grime and debris followed by dressing and polishing of the wheels and rims.

Clay bar decontamination - Clay bar treatment to remove tar, bug, and acid buildup from your car's paint.

Paint sealant - a synthetic coating is applied to the paint to protect against UV rays and oxidation.

Headlight restoration - Headlights are sanded down and refinished to improve visibility at night and make headlights look like new.

Engine bay cleaning - Full degreasing and dressing in the engine bay to make it look like new.
