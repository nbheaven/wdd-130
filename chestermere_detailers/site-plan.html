Content

Home page

Home page: Having a clean car is important. A recent study published that the average American citizen spends close to 2 hours in their car every day. At Chestermere Detailers we offer a comprehensive suite of interior and exterior services to make your car look and feel like it just rolled off the lot! Whether it's pet hair, stains and messes from children, scratched or sun beaten paint, we have the expert staff and professional grade equipment to make your car turn heads wherever you go. Interior services: The inside of your car is where you spend most of your time, and we remember that when working on every client's car. We offer the following interior services: Vacuuming - Removes loose dust and debris from all over the interior. Shampooing - Technicians use a specialized tool to spray then extract a heated cleaning solution into carpets and seats to remove stains, dirt, and odors. Steam cleaning - High pressure steam is used to remove grime from the inside of buttons and switches making sure they all feel like new, no more sticking. Leather restoration and conditioning - Our upholstery specialists will fix discoloration, cracks, and tears in your leather seats and trim. Odor removal - Powerful O-Zone emitters among other special equipment is used to remove odors from headliners, seats, carpets, and Hvac lines. Seat belt cleaning - Restore the spring in your old dirty seatbelt through a full extraction and sanitization process. Headliner cleaning - Special compounds and tools are used to gently remove stains and odors from your car's headliner. UV protection - A UV resistant coating is applied to the dashboard and all interior trim pieces to restore and protect them from the sun. We offer the following exterior services: Foam cannon and hand wash - a pre soak, followed by a foam cannon, then a two bucked hand wash leads to a perfect clean with zero swirl marks. Waxing - Full application of specialized wax to protect pain and enhance shine. Wheel and tire restoration - Full removal of brake dust, road grime and debris followed by dressing and polishing of the wheels and rims. Clar bar decontamination - Clay bar treatment to remove tar, bug, and acid buildup from your car's paint. Paint sealant - a synthetic coating is applied to the pain to protect against UV rays and oxidation. Headlight restoration - Headlights are sanded down and refinished to improve visibility at night and make headlights look like new. Engine bay cleaning - Full degreasing and dressing in the engine bay to make it look like new. Undercarriage cleaning - High pressure wash of the undercarriage to prevent rust and premature wear on suspension components. Glass cleaning and coating - Glass is decontaminated and treated with a hydrophobic coating to help with visibility in the rain and shine.

Packages

Packages and options page: Silver Package: Our silver package is perfect for those looking for a value oriented option to refresh the look and feel of your car. This package includes a full exterior foam cannon and hand wash followed by waxing and sealing on all exterior painted surfaces. The interior will receive a full vacuuming and wipe down as well as window cleaning and sealant. This package is great for regular maintenance and offers great results and value! Gold package: Our Gold package offers a full and premium experience. In addition to everything offered in the Silver package the gold package offers a full interior shampooing and leather treatment. Steam cleaning is also used to fix sticky or slow buttons and remove grime and dirt from places regular tools can't reach. Powerful tools are used to completely deodorize the entire cabin of the car. The exterior will receive the same treatment as the Silver package along with a full restoration to the tires and rims, a full undercarriage cleaning and paint sealant. Ultimate package: The very best we have to offer. In addition to everything that is offered in the gold and silver packages the ultimate package offers Seat Belt sanitization and cleaning, Headliner cleaning, and UV protectants will be applied to the dashboard and all interior trim. In addition to the gold and silver packages the exterior will receive a labor intensive clay bar decontamination treatment, headlight resurfacing and restoration, and a full engine bay cleaning and decontamination.
