Silver Package

Our silver package is perfect for those looking for a value oriented option to refresh the look and feel of your car. This package includes a full exterior foam cannon and hand wash followed by waxing and sealing on all exterior painted surfaces. The interior will receive a full vacuuming and wipe down as well as window cleaning and sealant. This package is great for regular maintenance and offers great results and value!

Gold Package

Our Gold package offers a full and premium experience. In addition to everything offered in the Silver package the gold package offers a full interior shampooing and leather treatment. Steam cleaning is also used to fix sticky or slow buttons and remove grime and dirt from places regular tools can't reach. Powerful tools are used to completely deodorize the entire cabin of the car. The exterior will receive the same treatment as the Silver package along with a full restoration to the tires and rims, a full undercarriage cleaning and paint sealant.
